## Visual Identity Notes

Adult man with fair skin, light gray-blue eyes, light eyebrows, clean-shaven face, rounded-oval face shape, broad forehead, soft jawline, straight medium nose, and a slight natural under-eye heaviness. Hair is light brown / dark blond with gray-blond highlights at the temples, combed back and to the side, with a small loose curl visible in the close three-quarter references.

Typical expression is calm, professional, and restrained; smile can be warm and open without becoming exaggerated. He wears a textured blue herringbone blazer or suit with a muted blue button-up shirt, no tie, no glasses. Overall styling: business portrait, calm studio lighting, dark navy curtain background.

## Prompt Fragment

Adult man with fair skin, light gray-blue eyes, clean-shaven rounded-oval face, broad forehead, soft jawline, light brown / dark blond side-parted hair with gray-blond temples and a small loose curl, wearing a textured blue herringbone blazer over a muted blue button-up shirt, calm professional expression, studio portrait lighting.

## Avoid

Do not add glasses, beard, mustache, black hair, very narrow face, sharp angular jaw, heavy wrinkles, tie, black suit, overly glamorous retouching, or exaggerated cartoon-like smile.
